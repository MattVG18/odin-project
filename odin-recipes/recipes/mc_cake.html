MC Cake Recipe

This cake is great for exploring caves or fighting zombies

Ingredients

  • 3 Buckets of milk
  • 2 piles of sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 3 bundles of wheat

Steps

  1. Gather each ingredient
  2. Go to crafting block
  3. Place the milk across the top row
  4. Place sugar, egg, sugar in second row
  5. Place the wheat in the bottom row
